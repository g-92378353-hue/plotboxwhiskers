Modul 5: Outlier Explorer

Kesan nilai luar biasa (pencilan) menggunakan julat antara kuartil (IQR).

Formulanya (Formula):

• Julat Antara Kuartil (IQR) = Q3 - Q1 = 35 - 15 = 20

• Had Sempadan Bawah (Lower Bound) = Q1 - (1.5 × IQR) = 15 - (30) = -15

• Had Sempadan Atas (Upper Bound) = Q3 + (1.5 × IQR) = 35 + (30) = 65
